About Earls Court Lifestyle Estate

The Estate is situated in the beautiful historic town of George surrounded by breath taking views of the Outeniqua Mountains and open grass fields, 5 km from the centre of town and 2 km from a shopping complex and Virgin Active. Earls Court is also close to schools, the George airport and Garden Route mall.

Earls Court Lifestyle Estate allows children to play carelessly, elder people to relax, fathers and mothers to go to work without stressing about the security at home. For this reason, our development is surrounded with electrical fencing or wall. Entrances to the estate are monitored by guards 24-hours a day and strictly controlled by a boom gate. Guards are telephonically linked to each residential unit. During the night, patrols are done at regular intervals by our guards on duty. Surveillance cameras are linked to the guards control room to provide a safe and secure environment. Special identity permits are required for workers to enter the estate.
