Your Forum Your Academy Welcome to our exciting new communication experience that will provide you with a range of learning and development options to support your Helpful Leadership Academy Experience and maximise collaboration between leaders across the Retail Business.

What is a Learning Forum? To put it simply this is a two way communication forum of the user, by the user, for the user! What you put in you will get out ten fold. Please take the time to share your tips, your ideas and your thoughts.

Leaders as Teachers Here you’ll find a one stop shop to support you on this programme. What it’s all about, its objectives and links to Helpful Leadership Academy modules. It will help you to give you everything you need to communicate powerfully with your people to encourage them to embrace everything we’re trying to do.

Look out for:-
- Regular briefings – from experts around the business
- Videos – where leaders and experts share their views
- Webinars – where you can find links to recordings on becoming the best communicators ever
- Hints and tips on secrets of success
- A chance to share and discuss your ideas on our comments boards

Please Get Involved This is your forum therefore we would love you to get involved whether it be through seeking or sharing helpful leadership tips and ideas to overcome challenges. We also want to hear about your success stories. What actions have you put into place following the workshop and how has this impacted upon your people or stakeholders and most importantly how has this enhanced the experience for the customer. We would also really value your feedback to shape a tool that genuinely supports your development.
