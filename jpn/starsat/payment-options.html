Payment Options

PLEASE NOTE: PAYMENT MUST REFLECT IN THE STARSAT ACCOUNT ON THE 1ST OF EACH MONTH TO AVOID DISCONNECTION. PLEASE BE SURE TO MAKE PAYMENT A FEW DAYS IN ADVANCE TO ENSURE RECEIPT OF PAYMENT. THIS IS ESPECIALLY IMPORTANT IF YOU BANK WITH ANOTHER BANK, NOT STANDARD BANK.

How do I pay my StarSat account?

  • Debit Order
  • ATM
  • Internet Payment (EFT)
  • Credit Card
  • Direct Deposit or (M65)
  • EasyPay Outlet

How do I pay via Debit Order?

Paying by debit order is hassle free!

Call the StarSat Call Centre (086 086 STAR / 7827) and we will automatically deduct the amount due for payment from your bank account every month on any of the listed dates available… (25, 26, 27, 28, 29, 30 or 1st of the month).

How do I pay via an ATM?

When paying at an ATM, make sure that you setup StarSat as a beneficiary on your bank account, so as to easily select it when paying at an ATM.

When making a payment, remember to use your Financial Account Number as the beneficiary reference in order to avoid account complications.

Use your Financial Account Number which is a nine digit number as the beneficiary reference in order to avoid account complications.

Please make your payment before the 1st of each month to avoid a disruption to your valuable viewing time.

How do I pay via Internet Banking?

StarSat is moving with the times, allowing the flexibility of paying your account through Internet banking via EFT. So now you can set up a recurring Internet payment via your personal bank account to StarSat.

On Standard Bank Internet Banking: Select StarSat from the list of pre–approved beneficiaries on your bank’s Internet banking website, and use your StarSat Account Number as the payment / beneficiary reference.

On Any other Banks – Internet Banking – Please load StarSat as a beneficiary:

  • Bank: Standard Bank
  • Branch Code: 019 205
  • Account Number: 252 315 553
  • Type of Account: Cheque

Use your Financial Account Number which is a nine digit number as the beneficiary reference in order to avoid account complications.

* If you do not bank with Standard Bank, please be aware that your payment may take up to 4 working days to reflect on your StarSat account.

Please make your payment before the 1st of each month to avoid a disruption to your valuable viewing time.

How do I pay via Credit Card?

Make payment via a credit card by calling the StarSat Call Centre (086 086 STAR/7827).

Please make your payment before the 1st of each month to avoid a disruption to your valuable viewing time.

How do I make a direct deposit?

You can now make payment at any Standard Bank branch.

Complete a deposit slip or the M65 printed on your monthly statement and make a payment directly at any of the Standard Bank branches. Please note that the M65’s will not be available at the branches, you will be required to cut them out from your monthly statement.

Payment via the branch, here are our account details:

  • Bank: Standard Bank
  • Branch Code: 019 205
  • Account Number: 252 315 553
  • Type of Account: Cheque

Use your Financial Account Number which is a nine digit number as the beneficiary reference in order to avoid account complications.

Please make your payment before the 1st of each month to avoid a disruption to your valuable viewing time.

How do I pay at an EasyPay outlet?

Designed for your convenience, you can pay your account at any EasyPay outlet nationwide.
