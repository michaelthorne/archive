Do you need a decoder?

StarSat will have a new HD decoder. Current or former TopTV subscribers can use their TopTV decoder to receive the StarSat signal. Or, if they prefer, they can buy the new StarSat HD decoder and use it together with their current TopTV satellite dish installation. The cost of the StarSat HD decoder ONLY (i.e. without dish & installation) is a reduced price.
