How to get StarSat

SMS ‘STAR’ to 40004 and we’ll call you back to arrange a full HD StarSat Decoder delivered to your home, or simply purchase a decoder from one of our retail partners. Or call 086 086 STAR/7827 for our store list.

SMS Registration

Register by calling 086 086 STAR / 7827 or SMS your South African ID number to 40004. A Call Centre agent will return your call within 72 hours to complete the registration process. You will need to have your StarSat decoder Smart Card number, SA ID number and your banking details available. (Banking details are required if you wish to pay by debit order for ease of payment and your convenience.)

Installation

The Call Centre agent will call an installer to make the necessary arrangements for your installation. StarSat will cover the cost of the standard residential dish kit and installation to a single TV point.

Any additional requirements and non–standard equipment is for the subscriber’s own account. A 3–month warranty on the installation is offered by the installer. Please ensure that you keep the installer’s details on hand should it be necessary to contact him within the 3–month period.

Activation

After your installation is complete, please SMS 40008 to activate your subscription or call the Call Centre (086 086 STAR / 7827). Please have the installer’s TTP number at hand when you do so, to speed up the process.

Payment Options

Your initial payment must be made within 24 hours from activation to avoid disruption to your viewing. Please use your financial account number as reference when making payment, for speedy, accurate reconciliation of your payment.

Payment options include:

  • Debit order – debit orders can be arranged within 15 minutes, through the Call Centre.
  • Direct cash deposits or EFT to Standard Bank – bank details on your statement.
  • Credit card
  • EasyPay (please use the EasyPay reference number available from our Call Centre).

If you sign a contract to pay your subscription for 12 months consecutively you will receive the 13th month’s viewing free. Please discuss this option with your installer.

Prepaid Option

StarSat has a Prepaid option, allowing you to buy a Prepaid voucher and view StarSat channels in the package that you have selected, to the value of the voucher. See StarSat Prepaid T&C’s on the StarSat website.
