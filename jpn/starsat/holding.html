About StarSat

StarSat is a digital satellite TV brand, operating in the South African market, offering a multitude of channels available in simple package choices for the subscriber. The channels include movies, news, series, kids programmes, music, gospel, documentaries, sport, lifestyle, telenovelas, and other. The packages are priced to give the subscriber good viewing value, and to make digital TV available to everyone.

StarSat is the brand that replaced TopTV, and was officially launched on 31 October 2013. The new packages and channels will be available on 1 December 2013. The new StarSat decoder is fully HD. Existing or lapsed TopTV subscribers will be able to receive the StarSat signal on the TopTV decoder.

TopTV

StarSat will have a new HD decoder. Current or former TopTV subscribers can use their TopTV decoder to receive the StarSat signal. Or, if they prefer, they can buy the new StarSat HD decoder and use it together with their current TopTV satellite dish installation. The cost of the StarSat HD decoder ONLY (i.e. without dish and installation) is a reduced price.
