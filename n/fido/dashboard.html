Where does Fido get its information?

Fido is powered by Experian one of the world’s largest credit bureaus used by the leading financial and retail institutions.

How often does Fido check my Credit Profile?

Once you’ve created an account, Fido will check your credit report automatically every month on your behalf and notify you of any changes to your credit status.

How accurate is Fido?

Fido can only access information that is available on the national Credit and Loan databases. Experian accesses this information and provides it to us. Fido uses the same info that lending and retail institutions access to determine your creditworthiness.
