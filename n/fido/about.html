Say hello to Fido

Fido is an easy-to-use credit check service brought to you by FRANK.NET and powered by Experian, one of the world’s largest credit bureaus.

Fido fetches your credit info for you and helps you make sense of the numbers. So, with your money’s new best friend, you can now look forward to regular credit reports that are quick, easy and free. Good Fido!

Things Fido loves

01

Playing fetch

Fido has partnered with Experian – a leading international credit bureau to give you instant access to your important credit information.

02

Being useful

Fido simplifies your credit report to show you the important info and help you get to grips with your credit report.

03

Barking

Fido keeps checking your report in the background and barks (via email) whenever there are changes to your credit report.

04

Wagging

Fido’s master Frank has is a nice fellow too. FRANK.NET will offer Fido customers treat and special offers from time to time.

05

Guarding

Fido will let you know if legal action or fraudulent activity takes place on any of your accounts – or if anyone but you tries to access your Fido account.
