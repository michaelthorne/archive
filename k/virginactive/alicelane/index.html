    Health club luxury, redefined

    Welcome to the Virgin Active Classic Collection, the world’s leading collection of luxury health clubs, designed to provide you with a personalised and inspired exercise experience.

    Our Classic Collection

    Your Alice Lane Health Club membership gives you access to Virgin Active Health Clubs’ network of 270 clubs internationally, with 16 clubs currently in our Classic Collection.

    This includes health clubs in London (UK), Lisbon (PT), Madrid (ES), Milan (IT), Sydney (AU) and Singapore (SG).

    Location is everything

    Alice Lane Health Club is located in the heart of the Sandton CBD, the financial hub of Africa, and just five minutes from the Gautrain. With a range of world–class amenities in walking distance, Alice Lane Health Club is poised to become a central feature in the business and social culture of this vibrant district.

    Rooftop training

    1. Enjoy the freedom of a fresh outdoor workout in our multi–functional rooftop training space.

    2. Here you will find a range of exciting all–weather training equipment, zones for outdoor classes, a 20–metre track and outdoor treadmills for that outside–run feeling without leaving the club.

    The Alice Lane training experience

    We are about delivering only the very best of what the fitness world has to offer. Expect the very latest training equipment, the most up–to–date innovations and technologies and the highest skilled fitness experts and personal trainers to guide you on your journey.

    Group Exercise

    Experience the latest innovations in the world of group exercise as expert instructors guide you through the group exercise activity of your choice in four dedicated studios.

    1. Boxing Jab away at your own punching bag in a group workout that combines strength, cardio and agility.

    2. Kettlebells A combination of weight and functional training with a wide range of motion and core focus.

    3. TRX Suspension apparatus that uses gravity and your own body weight.

    4. Pilates A dedicated Pilates Studio with a complete range of Pilates–specific equipment and accessories.

    5. Power Plate Accelerate muscle movement, increase strength and circulation and improve bone density.

    Ride with a view

    Experience the ultimate virtual indoor cycling class with MYRIDE, featuring breathtaking real–life scenery and detailed video coaching from the world’s best instructors.

    Anti–gravity® yoga

    1. Breath new life into your yoga practice with Anti–Gravity® Yoga.

    2. Anti–Gravity® Yoga combines the principles of yoga, Pilates and even dance. Work with gravity to stretch, strengthen and realign your body. Increase overall flexibility with less strain on your joints and vertebrae.

    The world’s most intelligent bike

    Combine the science of how you ride with the most authentic real–bike feel ever. The detail and accuracy of Wattbike’s data is at the forefront of indoor bike riding technology.

    Perfecting the art of movement

    Be amongst the first in the world to experience Technogym’s new Artis range. Superior form and function combine to create this range of cardio and strength equipment.

    The future is functional

    1. Think suspension, resistance, reaction and torso training.

    2. Queenax is the leader in functional frame design, creating a versatile space for multiple users. The structure is also fitted with boxing bags, for a solo boxing workout. Queenax units are located on the training floor and on rooftop.

    Health and wellness with a personal touch

    Access the very best experts in our Health Suite as part of your introduction to Alice Lane Health Club. Our biokineticists will develop a training programme around your health goals and our dietitians will personalise an eating plan to complement your lifestyle requirements.

    Your 2 o’clock is here

    Why go to a meeting when you can have it come to you? Our fully equipped glass–walled boardroom has everything you might need for an executive meeting, including conferencing facilities with printer, scanner and fax.

    I’ll be at the pool

    Our Poolside Lounge is a versatile space designed around comfort and function alongside our gleaming 20–metre, four–lane swimming pool. Enjoy a cup of coffee while on our iMacs, browse your iPad or catch up on current affairs on our 165–inch high–definition media wall.

    Fine dining or gourmet to go

    Take a seat in our cafè, with menu designed by Kohu’s award-winning in–house chef, skilled in the art of fusing healthy and haute cuisine. We’ve got a wide range of refreshing beverages from artisan beers to boutique wines, or why not enjoy the perfect cup of coffee at our Brew Bar.

    Shoeshine bar
